About

This chrome extension was developed by @BouazzaFerouk just for fun. Facebook Firewall v 1.0 is the most complete extension for facebook privacy. It help you protecting your privacy while using your favourite social network.

Features

  • Google Material design style.
  • Prevent "Seen" in chat box.
  • Prevent "Is Typing" in chat box.
  • New: Prevent "Last seen" when you access facebook.
  • New: Prevent "Is typing ..." while typing comments.

This extension might be updated in the future.
